PTC's Spatial Toolbox

 
During this past summer, 2020, I worked for PTC (located in Seaport Boston) through the Tufts Center for Engineering Education and Outreach (CEEO), and then eventually directly for PTC as an Academic Intern. During my time, I helped to integrate their app, Spatial Toolbox, with the SPIKE Prime LEGO Robot. The Spatial Toolbox is an Augmented Reality (AR) tool that allows the user to use visualizations to control various objects (such as the SPIKE Prime) in the real world.
× Through the use of HTML/CSS, JavaScript, and Python, my team and I were able to sucessfully integrate the app with the robot and presented our work to PTC. We also included more tools for the user, which allowed for a more diverse usage for the app. Some of these tools included the use of databases (Airtable), API's, and IoT. Below, is an example of our tools which also control the SPIKE (voiced by one of my teammates).

Mental Mentality

 
I began working on Mental Mentality with a few of my friends during the 2019 Polyhack at Tufts University, which is held each year. My group and I decided to work on a project that would mean a lot to us, and could help our peers at Tufts. We created a website (it is in demo version) that would allow students to make appointments with mental health services at Tufts.
× We created this website with the mission to allow students to more easily schedule appointments. We realized that Tufts' only method of scheduling an appointment was over the phone, which some students did not feel comfortable with. Students also reported that their inital meetings with therapists usually did not go well because they would feel pressured to go through their entire life story. Rate ur Memes

 
Rate ur Memes was a fun project that I worked on with my team during our Web Development class. This was one of my first large project where I had to put all my skills to the test. In this project, I utlized a lot of HTML/CSS, Javascript, and API calls to a software called Affectiva. Affectiva is an AI program that analyzes a user's facial expression.
× With this unique program, we created a website that would show the user a set of various memes and guage their facial expressions. We store these values such that other users can view the responses in a library which contains all of the various memes and gifs. The idea behind this project is that a company can use our website to measure their own content; they can then see how effective their adds or other forms of media are.
